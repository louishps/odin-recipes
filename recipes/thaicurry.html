Thai Curry

Delcious Thai Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Green Peppers
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Coconut milk
  • Thai Curry Paste
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Fry chicken with curry paste
  2. Add chopped green peppers
  3. Pour in coconut milk
  4. Add fish sauce and sugar
  5. Simmer for 10 mins whilst boiling rice
  6. Serve with rice
