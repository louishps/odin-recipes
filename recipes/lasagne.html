Delcious Lasagne Receipe

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Tomatos
  • Tinned Tomatos
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Beef Stock
  • Lasagne Pasta Sheets

Steps

  1. Turn on the Oven to 180 deg celcius
  2. Fry Beef and season
  3. Add all other ingredients
  4. Add to oven
  5. Cook for 1 hour
  6. Serve
